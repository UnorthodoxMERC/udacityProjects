Gorilla

A gorilla's bite force is incredibly strong, even stronger than a lion's!

  • Scientific Name: Gorilla Gorilla
  • Average Height: 5.6 - 5.9 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 35 - 40 years
  • Habitat: Tropical rainforrest of central Africa

Gorillas are large, powerful, herbivorous primates found in the tropical forests of equatorial Africa. They are characterized by their stocky builds, long arms, and large hands. Male gorillas, known as silverbacks, have a prominent patch of silver hair on their backs.
